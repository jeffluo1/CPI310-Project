EDWARD KIM: I am from the island of Oahu and came to Arizona for university. I am an Informatics Major in the focus area of Digital culture. Since I am from Oahu, Hawaii experiencing mainland was quite new for me. It was pretty overwhelming but now I think I got the hand of it. During my High school I focused a lot on my studies and also was where I noticed I wanted to partake in the major I chose for University.

In University I first started as a Computer Engineer major but then instantly noticed this was not what my passion was telling me to go with. This then lead to me changing my major to Computer Science. Since it tackled programming and I was very interested in that. However as my first semester was coming to a close in my sophmore year. I then noticed that this was not what I wanted to do. I wanted to be more creative and also still would like to be coding. This was when I looked heavily in the Majors given by Arizona State University and found the Informatics major. This was definetley what I wanted to do due to how flexible and somewhat free my flow chart was, but what really caught my eye was the amount of Web Development coding I am able to take, while focusing on the digital culture area that allows me to be expressive and creative in what I do.
