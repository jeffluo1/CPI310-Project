ANTHONY NGO: My Whole Life, I have resided in Arizona. Often, I seeo myself as an individual who came from very humble beginning. My parents imiigrated to America with a very common "Just the shirts on their backs" story from Saigon, Vietnam. Growing up, it seemed almost natural that hardwork and respect leads to good rewards and outcomes. A year after highschool, I found myself spending ten months going through training with the army to join the national guard. This would be my route to pay for college. Eventually I made it to ASU, where I found my curiousity for technology led to a passion in computer sciences.
